# Father's Heart Bible — SEO Keyword Strategy

> Working file. Kevin adds candidates anytime under **Candidates**. Claude runs SERP analysis and moves winners up to **Adopted**, losers down to **Skip / revisit**.

## How this file is used

1. **Kevin** drops new keyword candidates into the Candidates section anytime — no formatting required, just the term.
2. **Claude**, when asked, runs SERP analysis on each candidate, scores it on:
   - **Competition** — saturated / medium / open lane
   - **FHB fit** — how well our distinctive angle matches the search intent (1-5)
   - **Priority** — Tier 1 (target now), Tier 2 (target later), Skip (no fit / too saturated without a unique angle)
3. Winners get a **suggested blog angle** and move into **Adopted**.
4. Adopted keywords drive: future blog post titles, Sanity post `tags`, internal link anchor text, pillar-page content.
